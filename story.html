Our Story

A journey of love, laughter, and beautiful memories

2019

The Beginning

We first met at a mutual friend's gathering. David was instantly drawn to Aira's laughter, and Aira couldn't help but notice David's kind eyes. We spent the whole evening talking and discovered we had so much in common.

2020

First Date

Our first official date was at a cozy coffee shop. Despite the nervousness, we talked for hours about our dreams, families, and future aspirations. It was then we knew there was something special between us.

2021

Growing Together

We traveled to the mountains for our first trip together. Getting lost on a hiking trail turned into one of our favorite memories. We learned how to navigate challenges together and support each other.

2022

Meeting Families

This year, we introduced each other to our families. Despite the initial nerves, both families welcomed us warmly. Our family dinners became cherished moments filled with laughter and great food.

2023

The Proposal

On a beautiful beach sunset, David got down on one knee and asked the most important question of his life. Aira said "Yes!" with tears of joy. It was a perfect moment we'll cherish forever.
