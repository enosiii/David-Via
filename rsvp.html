Frequently Asked Questions

When should I RSVP by? +

Please RSVP by June 11, 2026. This gives us enough time to finalize seating arrangements, catering numbers, and other important details.

Can I bring a plus one or children? +

Your invitation will specify if a plus one is included. Please check your invitation details. Children are welcome and we have special arrangements for our little guests!

What is the dress code? +

We request our guests to dress in colors that complement our wedding theme. Gentlemen: Traditional Barong Tagalog. Ladies: Coffee Brown Formal Dress, Cocktail Dress, or Elegant Separates. Please see Wedding Party page for more details.

Is there parking available? +

Yes! Both the church and reception venue have ample parking available. Complimentary valet parking will be provided at the reception venue.

What time should I arrive? +

Please arrive at the church by 1:30 PM for the 2:00 PM ceremony. This allows time for seating and ensures the ceremony starts promptly.

Can I change my RSVP after submitting? +

Yes, you can update your RSVP until June 11, 2026. Please contact us directly if you need to make changes after submitting. After the deadline, changes may not be possible due to final arrangements.

Where should I send gifts? +

A gift table will be available at the reception venue. If you prefer to send gifts in advance, please contact us for shipping information. Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift of all!
