Install Wedding App

Add David & Via's wedding to your home screen for easy access

Get instant access to wedding details, countdown, RSVP, and more right from your home screen. No need to search for the website - everything will be just one tap away!

iPhone / iOS

1

Open in Safari

Make sure you're using Safari browser to view this website.

2

Tap Share Button

Tap the Share icon at the bottom of Safari (square with up arrow).

3

Scroll and Select

Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen" option.

4

Confirm & Install

Tap "Add" in the top right corner. The app will appear on your home screen.

Android

1

Open Menu

Tap the three dots menu in the top right corner of Chrome.

2

Select Install

Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app" from the menu.

3

Confirm Installation

Review the prompt and tap "Install" or "Add".

4

Launch App

Find the app on your home screen and tap to open.

Why Add to Home Screen?

  • Quick access to wedding countdown and details
  • Works offline once loaded
  • Receive notifications about wedding updates
  • No browser navigation needed
  • App-like experience with full-screen mode
  • Easy RSVP submission
